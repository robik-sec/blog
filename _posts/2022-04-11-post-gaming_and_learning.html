Yes, gameification works, at least for me. What have I learned today…. there are timers on linux, not only cron jobs. System timers can start services like cron jobs… but they are much harder to find. but again pspy finds all the thing running on the host…

How to get info about system-timers

systemctl list-timers -all

After that you can find the jobs in /etc/systemd/system if the services are readable, you at least see who starts them, and what is executed.
